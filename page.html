FAQs
Is Hertime PMS right for me?

Hertime PMS is best for women in their menstruating years seeking PMS-symptom relief and optimized hormone health. Hertime PMS soothes menstrual cramps, levels mood swings, eases bloating, supports normal cycles, and balances hormones naturally. 

If you experience more extreme period symptoms, try Hertime PMS+, our extra strength formula for serious support. It combats intense cramping, heavy flow, irritability, bloating, and low energy in women with more pronounced hormone imbalances.

How soon can I expect to see results with Hertime PMS?

Mixhers customers report optimal results after taking Hertime PMS daily for 3 cycles.

After 30 Days: Decrease in bloating, cramping, and PMS-related cravings.

After 60 Days: Continued decrease in bloating, cramping, and cravings. Improved mood, more regulated flow, and decreased fatigue.

After 90 Days: Decrease in fatigue, flow, cravings, bloating, cramping, and hormonal acne. Improved in mood, concentration, and physical performance.  

How often should I use Hertime PMS and at what time of the day?

Hertime PMS should be taken daily at any time for best results. As with all supplements, your body will respond to Hertime PMS best when it has built up in your system over time.

Is Hertime PMS sugar free?

Like all Mixhers products, Hertime PMS is all natural, hormone friendly, toxin free, gluten free, plant based, and has no artificial colors, flavors, or added sugar.

Is Hertime PMS safe for teens and preteens to take?

Yes, Hertime PMS is safe and meant for anyone in their menstruating years. We always recommend consulting with your doctor before starting any supplement.

Is Hertime PMS safe to take while pregnant or breastfeeding?

We do not recommend using Hertime PMS if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. We recommend Mixhers Prenatal+ for women before, during, and after pregnancy. 

Can I take Hertime PMS if I’m on hormonal birth control?

Yes. When you’re on hormonal birth control, you are more prone to nutrient deficiency. Hertime PMS has nutrients to help you maintain optimal hormone balance.

How does Hertime PMS compare to competitors?

Rather than masking period symptoms, Hertime PMS addresses hormone imbalance, the root cause of PMS symptoms. When taken in powder form rather than a capsule or gummy, our all-natural ingredients work together to help your body quickly and efficiently absorb the nutrients. 

Solutions like painkillers and heating pads may offer temporary aid, but Hertime PMS is a long-term solution that provides relief for the entirety of your cycle instead of for just a few hours at a time.

Why is Hertime PMS a powder instead of tablet or gummy?

Unlike tablets or gummies, our liquid delivery offers the advantage of rapid nutrient absorption. This is important because your body can’t use what it doesn’t absorb.

Can I mix Hertime PMS with other Mixhers products?

Mixhers supplements were made to mix. We hope women are creating customized supplement cocktails to meet their individual wellness goals. 

Here are some of our favorite Hertime PMS pairings:

  • Coconut Lime Hertime PMS  + Mint Limeade Greens

How do I know if I should be using Hertime PMS or Menopause?

If you are in early stage perimenopause and are menstruating regularly, then Hertime PMS is the best choice. If you are late stage perimenopause or menopausal, then we recommend transitioning from Hertime PMS to Menopause. You can also take both products if you choose; they are different formulations and both provide many hormone-balancing benefits.
